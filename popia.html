POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act) Policy

Effective Date: 15 July 2025

This policy explains how Erase Debt SA collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (“POPIA”).

1. Purpose of Processing

Erase Debt SA only collects and processes personal information necessary for the delivery of our services, legal compliance, and legitimate business interests. Information is processed lawfully and transparently.

2. Information Collected

  • Full name, ID number, contact details
  • Financial and employment information
  • Banking details for payment processing
  • Credit report and supporting documents
  • Correspondence and queries

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, improve, and manage our services
  • To comply with the National Credit Act and other applicable legislation
  • To process payments and communicate with you
  • To protect your rights and comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Personal Information

  • Your data may be shared with authorized employees, credit bureaus, attorneys, or regulators as required by law.
  • We never sell or share your information for marketing purposes without your consent.
  • All third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors) are contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Data Security

  • We use secure cloud hosting, encrypted databases, and strong internal access controls.
  • Only authorized staff with a need to know have access to personal data.
  • All breaches, if they occur, are reported in line with POPIA requirements.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

  • Right to access and correct your information
  • Right to object to processing or withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Right to request deletion (subject to statutory and business record retention obligations)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator: www.justice.gov.za/inforeg

7. Retention of Personal Information

  • Personal information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill our legal and business obligations, or as required by law.

8. Contact Us

To exercise your rights or for POPIA-related queries, email support@erasedebtsa.co.za or call 083 661 2791.
